Our Story

Frozen Bread started as a simple love for homemade sourdough and a desire to reduce food waste. We wanted bread that tastes incredible, lasts longer, and fits into busy modern life — without compromising quality.

So we created a small-batch frozen bread service that delivers fresh-baked loaves straight to your freezer. No preservatives. No mass production. Just real, honest bread.

Meet the Team

Together, we experiment, taste-test, bake at home, and deliver the loaves we personally eat every week.

How We Make Our Bread

1. Mix & Ferment

Slow-fermented dough using natural methods for maximum flavour.

2. Bake Fresh

Every loaf is baked in small batches, by hand, at home.

3. Freeze Immediately

Flash-frozen to lock in freshness and preserve quality.

4. Deliver Monthly

Reliable delivery right to your doorstep. Zero waste.
